mx::astro::astroSpectrum< _spectrumT, freq > Struct Template Reference

template<typename _spectrumT, bool freq = false>
struct mx::astro::astroSpectrum< _spectrumT, freq >

Class to manage an astronomical spectrum.

Details of the spectra, including units, file-location, and how they are read from disk, are specified by template parameter _spectrumT. Spectra are loaded and immediately interpolated onto a wavelength grid. This is to facilitate manipulation of spectra, i.e. for multiplying by filters, etc.
